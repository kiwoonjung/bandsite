The Bees Knees

The Bees Knees is a pop rock band originating

from San Francisco, California. The band

consists of lead vocalist Bryan Teddy, guitarist

Mack Tolkens, guitarist Andrew Green, bassist

Kent Zulkee and drummer Freddie Fischer. The

band achieved its first commercial success as

an unsigned act, drawing attention from

manager Cam Adams. Having worked with

other popular acts (The Elephant In The Room,

One Trick Pony, The Cat’s Meow), Adams was

confident the band could succeed even further,

pushing them to sign with Magenta Records.

From there, the band has soared, gaining

Finternational recognition and ranking in the

Ftop 10 on the Poster Charts. New fans

Fimmediately fell in love with the band’s original

Fand organic attitude, solidifying their place as

Fone of the hottest and fastest up and coming

Facts of this century. Fans affectionately refer to

Fthemselves as “The Hive” and are drawn to

Ftheir candid and authentic performances on

Fstage. Looking forward to touring in the future

FThe Bees Knees are currently recording their

Fsecond studio album, looking to be released in

Flate 2021.
